Vision & Mission Of College

“To offer affordable and inclusive education for all irrespective of cultural, gender and economic biases while equipping the students with desired skills, knowledge and attitude in their chosen stream, inculcating values, identifying hidden talents, providing the opportunities for students to realize their potential and thus shaping them into future leaders, entrepreneurs and above all good human beings”


Mission

  • To provide adequate learning opportunities in higher education to students hailing from rural areas.

  • To serve humanity through the creation of well rounded, multi skilled, globally competitive, employable, and responsible citizens

  • To impart quality education, integrating skill development as per Skill India Initiative of Government of India.

  • To foster awareness about social and environmental issues.

  • To ensure ample opportunities for extracurricular and academic advancement of students that contribute to the development of a holistic personality.

  • To create a supportive community where students, instructors, parents, alumni, and administrators can all work together.

  • To provide adequate sports facilities to students in order to hone the inherent physical strength that high landers are born with.

  • To develop networks and partnerships that will improve teaching, learning, extension, and job creation.

  • To channelize young energy by utilising their creative abilities, resulting in thoughtful and empathetic citizens.
